- **Decks in the Player (S4)** — rendered slide decks are now linked to a course and viewable by learners. New `DeckArtifact` model (tenant-scoped, RLS-registered; migration `e7a9c1b3d5f6`) binds a deck's `deck.{pdf,pptx,html}` R2 keys to a released `CurriculumVersion` (optionally to the source item). New learner-gated, enrollment-scoped `GET /api/v1/learn/courses/{enrollment_id}/decks` returns the pinned version's decks with **fresh presigned GET URLs** (cross-tenant/cross-learner → 404), plus an author-preview `GET /api/v1/slides/versions/{version_id}/decks` (architect/program_manager). The Course Player gains a "Slides" surface that embeds the HTML slides and offers PDF/PPTX downloads, gracefully absent when a course has no decks.
