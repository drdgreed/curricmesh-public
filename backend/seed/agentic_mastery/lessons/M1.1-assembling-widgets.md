## Overview

Assembling a synthetic widget is a pretend, three-step process:

1. Snap the sprocket into the flange.
2. Seat the flange onto the gizmo.
3. Give it an imaginary quarter-turn.

## Common Mistake

Forgetting the quarter-turn leaves the (fictional) widget loose. Since none of
this is real, no harm done — this is placeholder content.

## Summary
